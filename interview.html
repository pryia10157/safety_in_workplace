Interview With Person


Q: What are various types of communication media?

A: Various types of communication media are: Twitter, Facebook, Instagram and Cell phone.


Q: What are the advantages and disadvantages of communication media?

A: Well, the advantages of communication media are much faster and cheaper if you are using internet you and communicate virtually in real time, less need of social interaction. The disadvantages are people may not have access to internet or mobile service and people may not have experience using the media effectively and may be used for the wrong things.


Q: Why is health and safety important?

A: Health and safety are important because it protects the wellbeing of employees, visitors and customers, looking after. Health and safety make good business sense. Workplaces which neglect health and safety risk prosecution, may lose staff, and may increase cost and reduce profitability.


Q: What are some safety practices in the laboratory?

A: Certain substances should not be consumed, safety ware is needed and follow all instructions given by supervisor.


Q: What are some good healthy practices in the laboratory?

A: Wear clothing and shoes that cover exposed skin that protect you from potential splashes and no food and drinks allowed.
