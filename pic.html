OSHA

OSHA stands for Occupational Safety and Health Agency. This agency sets the health and safety standards in Trinidad and Tobago, in which all industries abide by to ensure the safety of their workers.

Safety Reports

All on work accidents, safety hazards and violations must be reported

Risk Management

The health and safety risk of each situation within a work space must be assessed and proper measures to ensure workers safety must be put in place.

Mask Distribution

Given the current pandemic workers who often engage with the public are at a high risk of infection by the Covid-19 virus. To reduce this risk masks should be distributed to workers.

Sanitization

All tables, chairs and equipment must be frequently sanitized to prevent the spread of diseases.
